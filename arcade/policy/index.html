Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Passage Arcade ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use the application.

2. Information We Collect

Passage Arcade uses Google OAuth for authentication. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information (name and avatar) from Google. This is used solely to identify you within the app and to enable API access.

We do not collect, store, or log any personal data on our servers. All data processing is performed client-side within your browser.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information obtained via Google OAuth is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your identity.
  • Grant the app permission to call the Google Gemini API on your behalf for generative AI features.

4. Google Gemini API

With your consent via OAuth, the app may access the Google Gemini API to provide AI-powered features. API calls are made directly from your browser to Google's servers. We do not intercept, store, or transmit your API requests or responses.

5. Data Storage & Sharing

Passage Arcade does not operate its own backend servers. No personal data is stored, sold, or shared with third parties. All authentication tokens are handled by Google's OAuth flow and stored locally in your browser.

6. Data Security

We rely on Google's industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocol for secure authentication. Since we do not store your data, there is no risk of data breach from our side.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at the app owner's email address listed on the GitHub profile hosting this site.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

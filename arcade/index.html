Passage Arcade

An AI-powered reading app that generates books from your prompts.

Read any fiction or non-fiction you like with our generative reading engine. Want a detective story, a horror novel, or a narrative non-fiction on the history of Egypt? Say no more.

Just type in your idea and the AI creates a full book outline for you. Then read your book passage-by-passage by tapping Generate — each tap brings the next part of the story to life. Change the story, bring in a subplot, or introduce a new idea anytime with the Pivot feature.

Features

  • Hand-tuned generation engine built for highly readable books.
  • Supports fiction, fiction sagas (series), and non-fiction.
  • Customizable prose style, prose pacing, and target length (short story, novella, novel, etc.).
  • Pivot the story or thesis anytime to create a new version.
  • Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) — use different AI models of your choice.
  • Great for genre fiction and light-weight non-fiction.
  • Export generated books to ePub to store and share with friends.
  • Available on Android and iOS.
